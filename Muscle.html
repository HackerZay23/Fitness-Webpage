Muscle Mass

What is Muscle Mass?

It's the amount of muscle in your body, including skeletal muscles, smooth muscles, and cardiac muscles. It can be measured
as part of your total body composition, along with fat mass and bone mass.

What are the benefits that come with Weight Loss?


  • Enhances your level of functional fitness: Accordingly, the higher your level of muscular fitness, the more likely you will be able to do tasks without undue fatigure or risk of injury
  • Facilitates your efforts to control your weight chronic fatigue
  • It helps improve cholestrol
  • It reduces blood pressure.
  • It improves mobility, According to the Arthiritis Foundation, losing ten pounds can take 40 pounds off your knees.
  • It improves breathing, by preventing obesity-related breathing conditions like obesity hypoventilation syndrom.
  • It reduces, risk of heart disease or stroke.
  • Inhances sleep-quality by lowering energy levels and increasing fatigue.
  • Improves your mood.
    Information written by ACSM Health and Fitness journal by James A. Peterson, Ph.D., FACSM
