Weight Loss

What is Weight Loss?

Weight loss is a decrease in body weight resulting from either voluntary (diet, exercise) or involuntary (illness) circumstances.
There are several methods when it comes to losing weight with the help of dieting and exercising.

What are the benefits that come with Weight Loss?


  • It boosts your energy level, by improving your respiratory functions and sleep quality
  • There's also a additional bonus to it which is , it can reduce your risk of chronic fatigue
  • It helps improve cholestrol
  • It reduces blood pressure.
  • It improves mobility, According to the Arthiritis Foundation, losing ten pounds can take 40 pounds off your knees.
  • It improves breathing, by preventing obesity-related breathing conditions like obesity hypoventilation syndrom.
  • It reduces, risk of heart disease or stroke.
  • Inhances sleep-quality by lowering energy levels and increasing fatigue.
  • Improves your mood.
